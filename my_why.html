Why

Here are some reasons why I want to be a web developer

  • I believe it to be the intersection of creativity and technicality, which I find fascinating

  • I am a natural problem-solver and I think with coding there are a ton of oppurtunties where I can use this skill and desire

  • I like processes and while there may be different ways to code things, the outcome is always the same. Provide an efficient, easy solution to the end-user

  • Great job outlook according to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics with a projected growth of 22% through 2030

  • Lastly, I think it's just fun! Yeah I may not get everything right away and sometimes it's frustrating but overall what I have been doing I really enjoy and love the challenge!
